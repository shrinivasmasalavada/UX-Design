Your Role
You are a junior UX designer at Lloyds Banking Group, a leading financial institution in the U.K.
You are part of a collaborative team focused on enhancing the user experience (UX) for digital banking customers.
Your team has been tasked with developing a new digital banking feature to compete with a competitor's recent market innovation.
As an aspiring UX designer, you see this as an opportunity to apply your research and design skills to real-world problems.
Your Goal
Your primary objective is to conduct a competitive analysis and gather customer insights to inform the development of the new feature.
You will research competitor offerings, analyse customer data to understand spending habits, and provide recommendations for improving Lloyds' digital services.
Your work will culminate in a Competitive Analysis Report detailing your findings and suggestions for feature enhancements.
